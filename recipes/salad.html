Apple Cranberry Walnut Salad

Description

One of my favorite lunch spots in San Francisco, Split, served a similar salad. I pulled this recipe and photo from Creme de la Crumb, but I would use a sharp white cheddar instead of feta! You also can replace bacon with celery to get a nice crunch.

Ingredients

  • Spinach and arugula
  • Apples
  • Walnuts
  • Celery (instead of bacon)
  • Dried cranberry or currant
  • Balsamic vinegar dressing

Steps

  1. Chop apples, celery, and walnuts (if necessary).
  2. Mix together in a big bowl.
  3. Add garnishes and enjoy!
