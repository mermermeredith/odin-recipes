Mocktail

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Seedlip Garden
  • 1/2 oz Seedlip Grove
  • 1/2 oz agave syrup
  • 3/4 oz lime juice
  • 2 drops saline/li>
  • Lemon wheel (garnish)
  • Mint (garnish)

Steps

  1. Shake all ingredients except garnishes together.
  2. Strain over ice.
  3. Add garnishes and enjoy!
